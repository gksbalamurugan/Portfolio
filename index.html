01. About Me

Beyond the Code.

I’m Balamurugan, an engineering student studying Electrical and Electronics Engineering at SASTRA University, passionate about building intelligent systems that interact with the real world.

I’m driven by curiosity and a desire to understand how things work beneath the surface. For me, engineering is a way of thinking — observing systems, questioning assumptions, and refining ideas until they become reliable and meaningful.

I believe the best learning happens not through theory alone, but through building, testing, failing, and improving.

I’m drawn to problems that demand patience, responsibility, and clarity — especially in areas like intelligent systems, automation, and mobility. My aim is to create work that is thoughtful, scalable, and genuinely useful.
